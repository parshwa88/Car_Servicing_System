 🚗 Car Servicing Management System

A Java-based Car Servicing Management System that allows customers to book vehicle service appointments and enables service centers to efficiently manage servicing operations. The system streamlines the booking process, tracks customer details, and manages different types of vehicle services.

---

## 📌 Features

- 👤 Customer Registration & Login
- 🚘 Vehicle Registration
- 📅 Service Appointment Booking
- ⏰ Time Slot Selection
- 🔧 Multiple Car Service Options
- 📋 Service History Management
- 📞 Customer Details Management
- 🔍 Search Customer Records

---

## 🚗 Available Services

- 🛞 Tyre Replacement
- 🛢️ Engine Oil Change
- 🔋 Battery Replacement
- 🛑 Brake Inspection & Repair
- ⚙️ Wheel Alignment
- 🔄 Wheel Balancing
- ❄️ AC Service
- 🚿 Car Washing
- 🔧 General Vehicle Servicing
- 🔍 Engine Check-up
- 💡 Electrical System Check
- 🚘 Suspension Inspection
